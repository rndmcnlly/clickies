Instructions & Tips

  • Step 3: Click "Create/Open Album Folder". This locks your machine selection for the session.
  • Step 4: Click "Start Recording Clip" to begin capturing video, inputs, and states.
  • Step 5: Interact! Click screen to lock mouse (ESC to unlock). Click "Stop" when done.
