Clickie Album Capturer & Riffer

A hybrid emulator/video system. Record your sessions, then play them back with the ability to jump in and take control at any moment.

üé• Phase 1: Capture

Start here to create your recordings. The Clickie Album Capturer runs the v86 emulator while recording video, input events, and machine states in the background.

Workflow:
  1. Create an empty "Album" folder.
  2. Record one or more clips of usage, referred to as "clickies".
  3. The system automatically saves .webm video, .savestream state data, and .webvtt input events.
Launch Album Capturer

üéÆ Phase 2: Riff

The Clickie Album Riffer is the playback interface. It looks like a video player, but it's running a "shadow" emulator synchronized to the footage.

Workflow:
  1. Load the "Album" folder you created in the previous phase.
  2. Select a clickie to watch.
  3. See something cool? Click "Take Control" to pause the video and instantly interact with the VM.
Launch Album Riffer
